# 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end banking customer analytics solution using Python and Power BI.

The objective was to understand customer demographics, banking behavior, financial patterns, customer loyalty, and risk profiles through Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and interactive dashboarding.

The project transforms raw banking customer data into actionable business insights that can help improve customer relationship management, marketing strategies, and financial decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Banks manage thousands of customer accounts containing demographic, financial, and transactional information.

Without proper analysis, identifying valuable customers, understanding financial behavior, and managing customer risk becomes difficult.

This project aims to answer business-critical questions related to customer profiles, deposits, loans, income, loyalty, and banking relationships.

# 💡 Key Business Insights

## 👥 Customer Demographics

- Most customers belong to a few dominant occupations.
- Banking services are primarily concentrated within specific customer age groups.

---

## 💰 Financial Behaviour

- Customers with higher estimated incomes generally maintain larger bank deposits.
- Higher income bands contribute significantly toward total deposits.

---

## 🏦 Banking Relationships

- Certain banking relationship categories contribute a disproportionately large share of total deposits.
- Loyal customers tend to maintain stronger banking relationships.

---

## 💳 Credit Behaviour

- Credit card balances vary considerably across income bands.
- Higher-income customers generally utilize larger credit limits.

---

## ⚠ Risk Analysis

- Customers with larger loans tend to appear more frequently within higher risk categories.
- Risk weighting helps identify customer segments requiring closer financial monitoring.
